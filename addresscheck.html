💡 What are confirmations?
Each new Bitcoin block (~10 min apart) adds one "confirmation" to your transaction. More confirmations = harder to reverse. Most businesses wait 1–3 confirmations for small amounts and 6 confirmations for large ones. After 6 (~1 hour) a transaction is considered effectively final.
